About Me

Kamruzzaman Chowdhury (Zaman) is an ambitious and passionate student currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Engineering at BRAC University, Bangladesh. With a deep interest in Artificial Intelligence (AI) development, he is steadily working toward creating innovative AI models, aiming to make significant contributions to the field. Zaman’s academic journey began at Jalalabad Cantonment Public School and College, where he spent 11 enriching years honing his skills in debating, public speaking, storytelling, and programming. His exceptional communication abilities and technical prowess have set a strong foundation for his future endeavors. Zaman is also preparing for international studies with a keen eye on Kyoto University of Advanced Sciences in Japan, where he plans to specialize in Electrical and Electronic Engineering. His fascination with Japan stems from its technological advancements, rich cultural heritage, and exemplary social ethics. Beyond his academic pursuits, Zaman is an enthusiastic Python programmer, steadily building his expertise in coding and problem-solving. He aspires to create ‘ZAMAN Labs,’ his own platform for sharing innovative projects and engaging with the tech community. Driven by curiosity and a love for continuous learning, Kamruzzaman Chowdhury embodies the spirit of a future innovator ready to make a lasting impact.
